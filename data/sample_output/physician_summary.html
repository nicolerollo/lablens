Interpretation notes

  • LabLens preserves the performing laboratory's reference interval from the source document instead of replacing it with a universal range.
  • Personal baseline uses median and IQR because they are less sensitive to outliers than a simple average.
  • Priority flags are descriptive signals for review, not medical advice.
  • Rows with ambiguous extraction, unmapped aliases, or unknown units should remain in a human review queue before use.
